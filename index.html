About Us

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate girls who train the younger generation for STEM projects and competitions. We provide opportunities for hosting workshops like CodeHers, Innovation Week, and more.

We bridge creative thinking towards real-life application, opening doors for girls in STEM to foster innovation. As the first ASA with an all-girls leadership team teaching STEM, we believe every girl deserves the chance to explore, create, and lead.

What We Do

Activities & Opportunities

From competition training to project-based learning, we offer a comprehensive STEM experience designed for girls who want to make an impact.

🏆

Competition Training

Get a general overview of STEM competitions and receive hands-on preparation. Complete project proposals that get assessed and refined.

📚

STEM Subject Help

Accelerate your regular studies and overcome academic challenges with targeted subject support from experienced senior students.

💡

Project-Based Learning

Solve real-world problems and innovate through hands-on projects. Design proposals, create posters, and develop solutions.

🎤

Presentation Skills

Develop and train your pitch and presentation abilities. Showcase your talents in various workshop opportunities and events.

🌏

Workshops & Events

Participate in workshops like CodeHers and Innovation Week. Connect with universities and access exclusive enrichment opportunities.

🤝

Service Opportunities

Give back by training the younger generation, mentoring peers, and contributing to the STEM community in Hong Kong.

Past Events

What We've Done

Look back at our successful workshops and events that have empowered girls in STEM across Hong Kong.

📚

CodeHers 2026

We hosted the CodeHers 2026 workshop, a hands-on event where girls explored coding, robotics, and design thinking. Participants worked on real-world projects, received mentorship from senior students, and gained confidence in their STEM abilities.

What Makes Us Unique

Why Join Us?

We are the first ASA to have an all-girls leadership team to teach STEM. Students will be able to approach unfamiliar STEM areas through the guidance of senior students who have been in their shoes.

✨ Our purpose is to provide a platform and regular sessions for girls who are passionate about STEM, where we can advise and offer competition opportunities to broaden their horizons. Whether you're exploring STEM for the first time or preparing for advanced competitions, there's a place for you here.
